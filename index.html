About

Hello!

I'm Reshma Mary Kurian, a passionate Ecommerce/Marketing Executive with a recently completed Master's degree in Business (Marketing) from the University of Wollongong Dubai. My journey in the field of marketing has equipped me with valuable insights into brand strategies, emerging trends, and innovative approaches.

I had the privilege of working with Unilever, one of the leading FMCG companies, in their E-commerce department, where I contributed actively to various aspects of the B2C realm. Collaborating with renowned retailers such as Carrefour, Amazon, Instashop, Elgrocer, and Noon, I played a key role in creating engaging content. Additionally, my responsibilities extended to supporting the global team by managing SKU details from around the world. In the B2B realm, I worked closely with the sales force and channel owners, facilitating orders through the USHOP platform across the UAE, KSA, Bahrain, Oman, and Qatar. Notably, I played a significant role in the successful relaunch of USHOP 2.0, an enhanced version of the previous website. I also had the opportunity to participate in the MENA Leadership Program at Mars Wrigley, where I engaged with renowned brands such as Galaxy, Snickers, Bounty, Twix, and Mars. In this dynamic environment, I contributed to various exciting projects focused on market innovation. Utilizing data analysis tools like Nielsen, RedSlim, Kantar, and EPOS, I tracked market growth and evaluated performance for the chocolate brands.This experience deepened my marketing expertise and provided insights into the intricacies of brand management within the fast-paced consumer goods industry. I was engaged in a stimulating position at Philip Morris International, focusing on sentiment management for customers and analyzing their journey to ensure satisfaction. In addition to analyzing customer feedback, I devised strategies to expand our customer base and maintained the happiness of our loyal clientele. During my time at Philip Morris International, I leveraged my marketing skills to contribute to customer satisfaction and strategic growth within the organization.
